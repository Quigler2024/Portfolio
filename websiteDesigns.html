Website Designs

Trinity Through Time is an interactive website designed to inform students about the history of Trinity’s campus in a way that has not been previously explored. The project showcases the rich architectural and cultural evolution of Trinity’s buildings, highlighting how the campus has transformed over time.

By combining historical research with engaging visual storytelling, the website makes campus history accessible, relevant, and compelling for a contemporary student audience. Ultimately, the project bridges past and present, allowing users to experience Trinity’s architectural legacy through an immersive and educational digital experience.

Enroute is a travel planning website designed to make organising trips more stress-free and efficient by tailoring itineraries to individual user preferences. The platform focuses on different travel styles, such as relaxing or adventure-based holidays, ensuring that each itinerary aligns with how users want to experience their trip.

As a prototype, the website features four popular tourist destinations (Dublin, NYC, Paris & Tokyo), offering users both fixed PDF itineraries and the option to create personalised plans based on the number of travel days. Enroute prioritises clarity and usability, allowing users to plan meaningful and enjoyable trips that suit their pace, interests, and expectations.
